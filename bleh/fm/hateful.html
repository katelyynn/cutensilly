Hateful content under bleh2

As of bleh2's first April 2024 update, the theme now by default hides hateful content from users.

Defining hateful content

For content to be blocked, it usually will be due to racism, transphobia, homophobia, nazism (in no particular order).

If this content being blocked is a problem to you, you were never forced to use the theme. But personally I believe those things listed (and any I missed out by accident) are far from helpful for any user and only promote hatred.

If you, yourself are listed, I hope you can make the change for the better if not now but in the future.

"This is censorship! Free speech!"

Sorry if you think this is too much, I like to try help people who really don't want to see this content, though ideally this would be last.fm's job. This is essentially a much lesser version of what last.fm could do if they cared.

The future

I do hope last.fm actually sticks to their word and implements at least a hate-speech filter, as even that is currently absent. One can hope.
