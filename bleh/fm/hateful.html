Hateful content under bleh²

As of bleh²'s first April 2024 update, the theme now uses a constantly updating list of hateful users in order to warn users of and hide hateful shouts, notifications, direct messages, and profiles.

This list is community contributed, and no single person is responsible for it all.

Hateful content usually falls under this extract from the Last.fm Community Guidelines (which they don't follow themselves but alas):

Be respectful to each other. [...] There is zero tolerance for predatory behaviour, disparaging or defamatory comments, threats, harassment, illegal activities, invading privacy, propaganda, racial hatred, [bigotry], offensive cultural behaviour, vulgar or obscene content, or other inappropriate behaviour or the revealing of other members' personal information.
Defining hateful content

This is by no means expected to have much of any positive effect, the Last.fm moderators are the ones responsible for fixing the mess they have caused. It's to be expected that running a site without a hate speech or spam filter will in-turn lead to a site filled with spam, harassment, and hate speech - like Last.fm says: "Use common sense".

If this content being blocked is a problem to you, you were never forced to use the theme. The users listed are listed for a reason and only promote hatred to the site.

If you, yourself are listed, I hope you can make the change for the better if not now but in the future.

Shouldn't you report these users directly to Last.fm?

Yes! - in fact, I (and many others) have for every single user listed. Have they been banned? 99% of them no.
