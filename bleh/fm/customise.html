Gendered music tagging
New

Hide gendered tags

By default, gendered tags are hidden in bleh² due to their unorganised and impossible nature.
Making use of the Similar artists panels and sites like rateyourmusic.com works in a much more related and organised way.

This includes (alphabetically) female, male, and nonbinary vocalist tags.
