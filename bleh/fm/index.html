Refreshing, sleek design for all occasions

Try out the new grid and icon-based interface, complete with full in-built range of light to oled themes.

Advanced text formatting

Remember back when last.fm supported text formatting in profile bios? bleh enables you to use markdown formatting (including line-breaks) in shouts and your own about me out of the box.


To users without the theme, line-breaks will show as regular spaces as last.fm chooses not to display them.

Tailored to your liking

The extension includes an in-built settings page offering a
colour customiser and accessibility options.


Your settings can also be exported to share and imported hassle-free.

In-built capitalisation corrections

Last.fm is a little slow at updating capitalisation corrections.. bleh stores in-built corrections for artists, tracks, and albums - with community contributed additions.

Including new, exclusive features

bleh includes a bunch of in-built additions (with more being added overtime). For example, here are saveable artist images, allowing you to keep track of images you like for later on.

New and improved aspects of last.fm

Ever thought the settings interface was lacking..? confusing? bleh has the solution.

Hiding hateful content

Hateful users are community-contributed and have their shouts and profiles automatically hidden, with the option to reveal shouts on a per-shout basis or globally.
