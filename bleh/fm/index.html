Features

Key features in bleh² to enhance your experience.

Refreshing, sleek design

Try out the new grid and icon-based interface, complete with a full dark theme.

Find the default theme too bright? Try out the OLED theme, available as an addon.

Customise to your liking

Users who post hateful content are automatically hidden - bleh² does what last.fm don't.
